Concept 2 Get-Togethers

hat it is: Regular community meetups—virtual and in-person—designed for connection, learning, and inspiration. How it works: Monthly online sessions with lightning talks, show-and-tell of works-in-progress, and open Q&A. Quarterly in-person walks: garden tours, nursery visits, and site walk-throughs hosted by local members. Rotating themes (pollinator power, dry shade, urban canopy) keep it fresh. Formats: Coffee & Canopy: 45-minute casual hang with one featured project and group critique. Field Fridays: On-site meetups to observe plant performance, maintenance, and seasonal change.Studio Swap: Member studios open their doors for demos of workflows, tools, and planting palettes.

  • Learn more

Concept 3 Render to Reality Labs

What it is: Monthly hands-on labs pairing visualization techniques with real planting strategies. How it works: Start with a shared scene file and prompt; participants iterate renders and then translate results into plant schedules, sections, and site details. Live critiques, before/after breakdowns, and published case studies.
